About Our Organization

" We the founders of Health Guru rehab takes an honor to bring to you a unique enterprise with the vison to serve the community and also maintain professional dignity and harmony in the field of rehabilitation. Our professional background began 20 years back in the field of Speech language Pathology and with a wide experience in the areas of, Early Intervention, home-based health services, neonatal feeding, acute medical settings, skilled nursing, and sub-acute skilled settings, as well as providing services in educational settings to make some changes in people's lives. "

Goal

Our goal is to provide total patient-quality care to maximize the patient level of function. We strive to provide the highest rehabilitation therapy services in any setting.

Competency

We strive to provide clinical-based services & a dedicated clinical programme based on client-centered quality care.

Growth

As healthcare providers, we prioritize outcomes and maximize the patient quality of life. We want to develop friendly employee and employer relationships, along with making a positive impact in Community Services.

Compliance

HealthGuru Rehabilitation is committed to serving the community & clients with respect, dignity, and integrity. We take an appropriate and comprehensive approach to comply with Federal & State Regulations.
